What is 'OpenID'?

OpenID allows you to have one identity to log into any OpenID-enabled website. There's no need to maintain separate credentials for every website you ever go on. I don't want your credentials. I just want to know that you are you and that's what OpenID does really well. I've created easy login buttons for some common OpenID providers. If your provider isn't there and supports OpenID, let me know. Also, tell CCP to be an OpenID provider.
